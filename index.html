BIO

History

Henrique Jardim fell in love with DJing at 16 when he watched FTampa, a Brazilian DJ, perform in his city. The vision of the DJ from a small city in Minas Gerais reaching the world made him believe he could get there too.

Since then, hours and hours of study and practice went by until he met several great souls, essencial to his formation and joined the electronic & sustainability collective Akatu Crew. A video from one of Futuristic Grow's performance in an Akatu Crew event found it's way to OVNI Records which then made Futurisc an invitation to be their representative in Brazil and later in Portugal.

Representing both Akatu Crew and OVNI Records label, Henrique traveled through several states in Brazil and now is touring through Europe. In a partnership with AstroFonik he's also playing Raggatek, a growing style in Europe and USA which he'll bring back to Brazil soon.
